How It Works

Agri Link keeps every order visible from the moment it is placed until the delivery is completed.

  1. 1

    Buyer Places an Order

    The buyer chooses the product, quantity, price, and delivery location.

  2. 2

    Seller Confirms the Order

    The seller checks the stock and prepares the products for pickup.

  3. 3

    Transport Delivers the Order

    The transport provider collects the products and delivers them to the buyer.
